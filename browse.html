Pizza Baked Potato
Prep: 1 hr 45 mins
Difficulty: Easy
Serves: 4

This recipe uses the microwave to bake the potato—a very quick method that will allow you to prepare baked potatoes for a weeknight dinner. But if you want to bake a potato in the oven, simply pierce the potato with a fork and rub it down with oil. Set the potato on a wire rack set over a baking sheet, which keeps the potato elevated and allows air to circulate all around for even cooking.
